Personal growth

One of my first assignments in the course i 1Talent was making a DISC test. A DISC test determines your personal profile. The possible results of the test are the four DISC behavioural styles dominant, influential, stable and conscientious.
Making this test was relevant because it allowed me to find out what kind of person I am within a team. I am an influence and a dominant type, which turned out to be the case after the test. On the basis of the results, teams were then formed, wich resulted in groups of different personalities. A situation that occurs frequently in daily life and certainly on the shop floor. My DISC personality typing
